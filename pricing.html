Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get listed?

We review all submissions within 24 hours. Once approved, your listing goes live immediately and starts appearing in search results.

Can I cancel my Pro or Premium plan anytime?

Yes — no contracts. Cancel through your account dashboard at any time and your listing downgrades to Free at the end of the billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. We do not store card numbers — all billing is handled securely by Stripe.

How do Featured listings appear differently?

Featured (Pro) listings appear at the top of search results with an orange Featured badge, enhanced card styling, and are rotated on the homepage. Premium listings lock in the first position.
